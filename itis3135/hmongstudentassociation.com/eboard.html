Meet the HSA Executive Board

Learn about the leadership team that guides the Hmong Student Association at UNC Charlotte.

Who We Are

The HSA Executive Board (Eboard) is dedicated to promoting Hmong culture, supporting members, and creating meaningful experiences on campus. Each position contributes unique skills to lead events, workshops, and community initiatives.

President

Alex Lee – Oversees all operations, represents HSA, and coordinates board meetings.

Vice President

Sophia Nguyen – Assists president and manages club initiatives and events.

Secretary

Michael Xiong – Maintains records, communications, and meeting notes.

Treasurer

Linda Chang – Manages finances, budgeting, and fundraising efforts.

Events Coordinator

Kevin Thao – Plans and executes cultural, social, and educational events.

HSA Bylaws & Rules

  • All members are expected to participate actively in events and meetings.
  • Eboard positions are elected annually by general members.
  • Decisions regarding events and budgets must be approved by majority vote of the Eboard.
  • Members must foster a respectful and inclusive environment.
  • All activities should align with HSA's mission of promoting Hmong culture and community engagement.
