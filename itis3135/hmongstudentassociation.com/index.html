Our Mission

The Hmong Student Association (HSA) at UNC Charlotte is dedicated to promoting awareness and appreciation of Hmong culture, traditions, and history. We strive to foster a welcoming and supportive community for Hmong students while connecting with students from all backgrounds who are interested in learning more about our heritage. HSA provides opportunities for engagement through cultural events, educational workshops, leadership programs, and social activities that encourage collaboration, personal growth, and meaningful connections on campus. By celebrating Hmong culture and empowering students to take active roles in campus life, HSA aims to create a vibrant, inclusive, and lasting impact on the university community.
