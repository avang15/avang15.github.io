How HSA Was Founded

The Hmong Student Association (HSA) was founded at UNC Charlotte in 2010 to provide a welcoming space for Hmong students and to promote awareness of Hmong culture across campus. By 2012, HSA had hosted its first annual Hmong New Year celebration, and over the following years, it organized cultural workshops, community service events, and social activities to connect students and celebrate Hmong heritage. In 2018, the organization expanded its membership and began collaborating with other student groups and local Hmong community organizations to increase cultural awareness on campus.
