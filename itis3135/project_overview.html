Project Overview: HSA Website Design

Basic Information

My application will be for UNC Charlotte's Hmong Student Association President. The main purpose will be to market the organization's events and opportunities for its members and the local campus community. The platform can also be used when outreaching to community organizations and other campus people groups.

The intended users will be anybody involved with or interested in the organization.

The website will include five key pages:

  1. Home/Introduction Page
  2. Eboard Positions Page
  3. History Page
  4. Upcoming Events Page
  5. Contact and Membership Sign Up Page

Individual Page Designs

All pages will include navigation bar.

1. HSA Home Page

  • Purpose: This page serves as an introduction for students to learn about the mission of HSA and who they are.
  • Audience: Campus Students and Org Members
  • Content: HSA Main Photo, Organization Mission, Organization Statistics
  • No Data will be collected.

2. Eboard Positions Page

  • Purpose: Page displaying eboard info and structure of the organization
  • Audience: This page is for people interested in learning about the eboard positions that exist in the organization
  • Content: Eboard Photo and Intro, Individual Positions and Description, HSA Bylaw/Rules

3. History Page

  • Purpose: This page is to tell people the history of HSA and the things the org has achieved
  • Audience: People unfamiliar with HSA and their history
  • Content: Creation of HSA, Interactive Photo Gallery of Past Events, List of Achievements
  • Includes arrow or sliding mechanism for scrolling through images

4. Upcoming Events Page

  • Purpose: Inform members of upcoming events and their details
  • Audience: Members and non-members looking to attend events and collaborations
  • Content: Interactive Event Categories Filter, 4-5 Event Listings
  • Includes buttons for interactions

5. Contact and Membership Sign Up Form

  • Purpose: To connect site viewers to HSA through a contact and member sign-up form
  • Audience: People looking to become members or have questions unanswered by site contents
  • Content: form for questions and membership information, additional methods of contacting HSA
  • This page will process data from users through a form and then deliver a completion page upon submission outlining the form data.

Dynamic Functionalities

Interactive Photo Galley: will be placed on the History Page. Should allow site viewers to scroll through images and blow them up to view and image caption. Example url: Photography Gallery

Interactive Event Filter: will be placed on the Upcoming Events Page. Will allow site viewers to filter events based on specific categories and hide certain Information depending on the chosen filter.Example url: Facebook Marketplace

Contact and Membership Form: will be placed on the Contact Page. Should allow users to fill out a form with information and then submit the form. Upon submission, the user will see a completion page that outlines the inputted data for user reference. Example url: The Sojourney Co.
